Dan Dan Noodles

Description

Quite possibly one of the best Sichuan dishes to ever live! This is a Taiwanese take from the Chef George Lee. Nothing quite like a spicy, nutty umami bowl of warm noodle goodness on a cold winter day.

Ingredients (For two)

  • 4 cloves garlic, grated
  • 2 tablespoons chili flakes
  • 1 tablespoon sesame seeds
  • 2 teaspoons ground Sichuan pepper
  • 4 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 4 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons dark brown sugar
  • 1.5 tablespoons peanut butter (or sesame paste)
  • 2 teaspoons black vinegar
  • 3 scallions, chopped to your preference
  • 10-12oz of your favorite noodles

Steps

  1. Put the garlic, chili flakes, sesame seeds, and Sichuan pepper into a heatproof bowl.
  2. Heat the vegetable oil in a small skillet over medium heat.
  3. Pour the oil over the mixture in the bowl and mix.
  4. Mix in soy sauce, brown sugar, peanut butter, and vinegar. Your sauce it finished!
  5. Boil your noodles following the instructions on the package.
  6. Put 4-5 tablespoons of your sauce in a bowl, along with about 1/4 cup of noodle water. Top with noodles and mix together.
  7. Top with chopped scallions. Enjoy!
